FX + EQ

Now that you have a basic idea of how Traktor works with loading and playing decks lets look at how we can improve the transition between tracks.

Using the EQ knobs (High, Mid and Low) you can cut out certain frequencies of a song. This can be useful when transitioning two songs with heavy low end frequencies. You can slowly drop out the low frequencies of one song whilst bringing up the low frequencies in the new track.

Another use for EQ knobs can be layering two songs over each other. This could involve cutting out the highs of one song and having only the highs of another song to create some sick transitions.

The looping knob allows you to loop certain parts of a song. You can adjust how many bars it loops by turning the knob. To activate the loop press down on the knob. Once you are finished with the loop you press the knob again and the song will resume as normal. Looping is good for longer transitions between songs and allows you to build atmosphere by dragging out the transition.


The effects (FX) within Traktor provide some extra depth to a mix. Using something like reverb can help you fade out a song without a sudden stop.
