Basics

So now that we have everything set up and working we can start mixing. First we will look at the music browser.

The file browser allows you to find that perfect song you want to mix. Traktor natively supports your iTunes playlists but also has the ability to create your own playlists inside Traktors software.

You can order your tracks by any of the meta data tabs - title, artist, year, date added, key, genre etc.

Once you have found your track load it into one of the track by either click and dragging or hitting load on your MIDI device. When loading the track for the first timed Traktor will analyse the track to two things, the BPM (beats per minute) and the key. The key tag allows you to mix two tracks of the same key together to get a seamless and in key transition between tracks.

Now load another track to mix with the first one. Now that you have two tracks loaded you can press play and get the music going.

Using the cross fader and the volume faders you can control how loud each track is playing. If you are not getting any sound make sure your gain is turned up for each deck.
