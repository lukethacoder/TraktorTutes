EASY SET UP

Technology has come along way since the old vinyl DJing set ups and is now even easier to set up. MIDI controllers are the best type of DJ equipment for beginners and you dont even need to be technologically gifted to set them up.

EASY TO LEARN

Even as a beginner the Traktor software is really easy and intuitive. Within a few minutes you can be mixing to a very basic level. Withing a few hours of practice you can be experimenting with cool effects and cue techniques.

ANYTIME - ANYWHERE

With Traktor Pro you can mix anywhere, anytime. Whether you have your MIDI controller or not your can mix your favourite tracks for friends at a party or smash some beats together on the bus. Your mixing goes everywhere
